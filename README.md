# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Strategic Business Recommendations

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on **customer segmentation** using data analysis techniques to group customers based on their characteristics such as income, age, and insurance preferences.

The goal is to help businesses **understand customer behavior** and design **targeted marketing strategies** for each segment.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Perform customer segmentation using clustering techniques
- Identify distinct customer groups
- Provide actionable **business strategies** for each segment
- Improve marketing efficiency and customer engagement

## 🧠 Customer Segments & Business Strategies

### 🔹 Cluster 0: Budget Conscious Customers
**Characteristics:**
- Low income
- Low insurance premium

**💡 Strategy:**
- Use **SMS campaigns** for cost-effective outreach
- Offer **basic and affordable insurance plans**
- Provide **discounts and promotional offers**

### 🔹 Cluster 1: Young Professionals
**Characteristics:**
- Medium income
- Younger age group

**💡 Strategy:**
- Focus on **digital advertising (social media, Google Ads)**
- Promote **investment-linked insurance products**
- Target users through **mobile apps and online platforms**

### 🔹 Cluster 2: High Value Customers
**Characteristics:**
- High income
- High insurance coverage

**💡 Strategy:**
- Offer **premium insurance plans**
- Provide **wealth management bundles**
- Assign **dedicated relationship managers** for personalized service

### 🔹 Cluster 3: Senior Customers
**Characteristics:**
- Older age group
- Stable income

**💡 Strategy:**
- Promote **health insurance plans**
- Offer **retirement and pension plans**
- Use **direct marketing channels** (email, postal communication)

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- Python 🐍
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Scikit-learn
- Matplotlib / Seaborn

## 📈 Key Outcomes
- Clear identification of **4 distinct customer segments**
- Improved understanding of customer needs
- Data-driven **marketing and business recommendations**

Customer segmentation enables businesses to move from **generic marketing** to **personalized strategies**, improving both customer satisfaction and profitability.
